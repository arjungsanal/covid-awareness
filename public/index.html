how to prevent virus

wash your place

Keeping your surrounding clean an disinfected is one of the key step in prevention

maintain distance

Maintain social distancing, by which we could reduce the chances of getting infected thus staying safe.

don't touch face

Touching your face without properly cleaning them could easily lead to the transmission of the virus.

wash your hand

Keeping your hands clean by washing them in regular intervals will help you stay free from the deadly virus

use napkin

Using a napkin while sneezing even within your house so that we could reduce its spread within the surrounding.

wear a mask

Wearing a mask is the most important step in the fight against COVID-19, thus keeping all of us safe.

things you should DO

wash your hand

Washing your hands in regular intervals helps to reduce chances of infection.

always wear a mask

Wearing a a mask helps us to avoid direct exposure to the deadly virus.

stay home during fever

fevers are one of the preliminary symptom of COVID 19 so stay under isolation and prevent its spread.

things you should NOT DO

avoid close contact with animals

Animals are also vulnerable to this deadly virus, staying in contact with animals also increases the chances of infection.

avoid close contact with peoples

Maintain social distance and avoid the chances of spreading of this virus, even the slightest carelessness can cause huge damage.

avoid crowded area

Crowds are the key mode by which this virus spreads so avoid crowds to avoid covid.
